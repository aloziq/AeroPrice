Understanding the Flight Dataset

The core challenge is modeling how various variables affect airline pricing in India. Flight tickets are highly dynamic, changing based on features like airline brand, timing, destination, and purchase urgency.

✈️

300k+ Records Collected

Covers 50 days of flight schedules between top metro hubs in India (Delhi, Mumbai, Kolkata, Chennai, Bangalore, Hyderabad).

💰

Drastic Class Disparity

Economy tickets range from ₹1,105 to ₹42,320, whereas Business Class spans from ₹12,000 up to ₹114,900, creating a massive predictive shift.

Preprocessing Pipeline

Feature Engineering & Transformations

To extract maximal predictive performance from tabular fields, raw values are passed through scaling and custom feature extraction pipelines before feeding the models.

01
Continuous Feature Scaling

Applied StandardScaler to center and scale numeric variables (days left, duration, stops).

02
Categorical Mapping & Encoding

Mapped Ordinals (Stops, times of day) directly. One-hot encoded nominals (airlines, source, destination).

03
Urgency Buckets (Booking Window)

Engineered booking_window: 0-7 days (extreme urgency), 8-14 days, 15-30 days, 31-60 days, 60+ days.
